Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 16, 2026

Information we collect

When you submit a quote request, we collect the information you provide, including name, phone number, email address, moving addresses or areas, move date, move size, and move details.

How we use information

We use quote request information to respond to your inquiry, estimate your move, schedule services, prevent spam, and maintain operational records.

Storage and access

Quote requests are stored in the website database and may be emailed to the business when notifications are configured. Access should be limited to authorized staff.

Security

The website uses server-side validation, rate limiting, security headers, restricted static file serving, and optional Turnstile spam protection. No website can guarantee absolute security.

Third-party services

The site may use an email provider for notifications and Cloudflare Turnstile for spam prevention if configured.
